# pickle-resources

## Purpose
The best Australian teaching resources available to primary school teachers — websites, tools, publications, and organisations. PickleNickAI references these to recommend resources, cite sources, and point teachers to the right support.

**This is not teaching knowledge — it is RESOURCE knowledge. Where to send teachers when they need help.**

---

## CURRICULUM RESOURCES

### AC9 Australian Curriculum (Official)
- **URL:** https://www.australiancurriculum.edu.au
- **What:** The official Australian Curriculum v9 — all learning areas, all year levels
- **Use:** Verify AC9 codes, download content descriptors, read elaborations
- **AC9 Resource:** https://ac9.edu.au — EasedUP version (easier to navigate)

### SCSA — School Curriculum and Standards Authority (WA)
- **URL:** https://www.scsa.wa.edu.au
- **What:** WA-specific curriculum support, WACE, assessment resources
- **Use:** WA teachers, WACE advice, sample assessments, teachers materials
- **Note:** SCSA blocks many bots — recommend teachers access directly

### TAA — Teacher Advisory Australia
- **URL:** https://www.teachstarter.com
- **What:** Themed resources, worksheets, unit plans, printables
- **Use:** Quick supplementary resources, homework, assessment tasks
- **Cost:** Free + Premium tier

## ASSESSMENT RESOURCES

### ACER — Australian Council for Educational Research
- **URL:** https://www.acer.org
- **What:** PAT tests, research, professional learning
- **Products:** PAT-R, PAT-M, PAT-Science, PAT-Listening
- **Use:** Understand PAT scaled scores, access research on assessment

### Brightpath
- **URL:** https://www.brightpath.com.au
- **What:** Moderated writing and numeracy assessments (WA)
- **Use:** Benchmarking, rubrics, moderated assessment
- **Note:** WA-based but used across Australia

### NAPLAN
- **URL:** https://www.nap.edu.au
- **What:** Official NAPLAN information, sample tests, data
- **Use:** Understand NAPLAN bands, find practice tests

### Victorian Curriculum and Assessment Authority (VCAA)
- **URL:** https://www.vcaa.vic.edu.au
- **What:** Victorian curriculum resources (useful even for non-Victorian teachers)
- **Use:** Sample assessments, annotated work, rubrics

## PEDAGOGICAL RESEARCH

### AITSL — Australian Institute for Teaching and School Leadership
- **URL:** https://www.aitsl.edu.au
- **What:** Australian Professional Standards for Teachers, evidence base
- **Use:** AITSL standards alignment, professional growth, registration
- **Key docs:**
  - Australian Professional Standards for Teachers (full)
  - National Review of Teacher Registration
  - Quality Teaching Initiative

### EEF — Education Endowment Foundation (UK but valuable)
- **URL:** https://educationendowmentfoundation.org.uk
- **What:** Evidence-based teaching strategies, toolkit (very high quality research)
- **Use:** Teaching strategies with effect sizes, what works in education
- **Note:** UK context but highly relevant — highest-quality education research globally

### Evidence for Learning
- **URL:** https://www.evidenceforlearning.com
- **What:** Australian evidence-based teaching resources
- **Use:** Localised evidence, teaching toolkit, professional learning

### COAG — Education Council
- **URL:** https://www.educationcouncil.edu.au
- **What:** National education policy, Alice Springs (Mparntwe) Declaration
- **Use:** Education goals for Australian students, Melbourne Declaration

## WRITING AND LITERACY

### Words Their Way (Supplementary)
- **What:** Phonics and word study program
- **Use:** Spelling instruction, word sorts, guided word study

### CAFÉ Reading (Supplementary)
- **What:** Comprehension strategy framework (Tompkins)
- **Strategies:** Comprehension, Accuracy, Fluency, Expanding vocabulary

### Seven Steps to Writing Success (Australia)
- **URL:** https://www.petersoakley.com.au/the-seven-steps-to-writing-success
- **What:** Explicit writing instruction, persuasive, narrative, informative
- **Use:** Writing units, Seven Steps methodology

### Talk for Writing (Australia adaptation)
- **What:** Imitation, innovation, independent writing framework (Pie Corbett)
- **Use:** Oral language before writing, text-maps, internalising text structures

### First Steps in Literacy (WA)
- **What:** WA DOE comprehensive literacy framework
- **Components:** Speaking, listening, reading, writing, viewing, representing
- **Use:** Diagnostic tools, literacy progressions, intervention planning
