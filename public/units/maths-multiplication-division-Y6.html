    • WEEK 1 — ENGAGE

    Focus: Cold Task + Integer multiplication

    5E Phase: Engage

    LESSON 1.2 — Rules for Integer Signs

    Duration: 50 minutes

    Type: Concept Introduction

    Learning Intentions

  • I am learning the sign rules for integer multiplication
  • I am learning: positive × positive = positive; negative × negative = positive; positive × negative = negative

    • Success Criteria

  • I can apply sign rules to multiply integers

    • I DO (15 min)

    "Sign rules for multiplication:"
    "Positive × Positive = Positive: 3×4=12"
    "Negative × Negative = Positive: (-3)×(-4)=12"
    "Positive × Negative = Negative: 3×(-4)=-12"
    "Negative × Positive = Negative: (-3)×4=-12"
    "Same signs → POSITIVE. Different signs → NEGATIVE."

    YOU DO (15 min)

  • 5×(-3)=___ (-4)×7=___ (-6)×(-5)=___
  • (-2)×9=___ 8×(-3)=___ (-7)×(-8)=___
  • "If 3×(-4)=-12, what is (-3)×4?"
  • EXTENSION: "Prove: (-2)×(-2)×(-2) = -8"

    • LESSON 1.3 — Integer Multiplication Strategies

    Duration: 50 minutes

    Type: Guided Practice

    I DO (12 min)

    "(-3)×(-4): same sign → positive. 3×4=12. Answer: 12."
    "(-5)×(-6): same sign → positive. 5×6=30. Answer: 30."
    "(-7)×8: different signs → negative. 7×8=56. Answer: -56."

    YOU DO (15 min)

  • (-12)×3=___ 12×(-3)=___ (-12)×(-3)=___
  • (-8)×(-7)=___ 9×(-5)=___ (-6)×8=___
  • "If a×b is negative, what does that tell you about a and b?"
  • EXTENSION: "What is (-1)×(-1)×(-1)×(-1)?"

    • WEEK 2 — EXPLORE

    Focus: Integer division

    5E Phase: Explore

    LESSON 2.1 — Integer Division

    Duration: 50 minutes

    Type: Explore

    Learning Intentions

  • I am learning that integer division follows the same sign rules as multiplication
  • I am learning that division is the inverse of multiplication

    • Success Criteria

  • I can divide integers using sign rules

    • I DO (15 min)

    "Division sign rules are the SAME as multiplication:"
    "Positive ÷ Positive = Positive: 12÷3=4"
    "Negative ÷ Negative = Positive: (-12)÷(-3)=4"
    "Positive ÷ Negative = Negative: 12÷(-3)=-4"
    "Negative ÷ Positive = Negative: (-12)÷3=-4"
    "Because: if 3×4=12, then 12÷3=4 and 12÷(-3)=-4 and (-12)÷(-3)=4."

    YOU DO (15 min)

  • 18÷(-3)=___ (-18)÷3=___ (-18)÷(-3)=___
  • 24÷(-4)=___ (-24)÷6=___ (-24)÷(-4)=___
  • "If 5×(-3)=-15, what is (-15)÷(-3)? What is (-15)÷5?"
  • EXTENSION: "What is 0÷(-5)? What is (-5)÷0?"

    • WEEK 3 — EXPLORE

    Focus: BEDMAS with integers

    5E Phase: Explain

    LESSON 3.1 — BEDMAS with Integers

    Duration: 50 minutes

    Type: Explain

    I DO (15 min)

    "(-3) + 4×(-2)."
    "BEDMAS: multiply before add. 4×(-2)=-8."
    "(-3) + (-8) = -11."
    "(-5) - (-3)×4."
    "Multiply: (-3)×4=-12."
    "(-5) - (-12) = (-5) + 12 = 7."

    YOU DO (15 min)

  • 6 + (-3)×2 = ___ (6 + (-6) = 0)
  • (-4) + 5×3 = ___ (-4 + 15 = 11)
  • 8 - (-2)×3 = ___ (8 - (-6) = 8 + 6 = 14)
  • EXTENSION: "(-2)³ - 3×(-4)"

    • WEEK 4 — EXPLORE

    Focus: Expanding and factorising algebraic expressions

    5E Phase: Explain

    LESSON 4.1 — Expanding

    Duration: 50 minutes

    Type: Explain

    I DO (15 min)

    "3(x + 4) = 3x + 12."
    "(-2)(x - 5) = -2x + 10."
    "5(-x + 3) = -5x + 15."
    "When distributing a NEGATIVE: watch the signs!"
    "(-3)(x + 2) = -3x - 6. NOT -3x + 6!"

    YOU DO (15 min)

  • 4(x + 5) = ___ 3(x - 7) = ___
  • (-2)(x + 4) = ___ (-5)(x - 2) = ___
  • 6(-x + 3) = ___
  • EXTENSION: "Expand: 3(x - 2) - 2(x + 4)"

    • LESSON 4.2 — Factorising

    Duration: 50 minutes

    Type: Explain

    I DO (12 min)

    "Factorise: 6x - 9 = 3(2x - 3)."
    "Common factor: 3. Divide each term by 3: 6x÷3=2x, 9÷3=3."
    "(-4x + 6) = -2(2x - 3). Note: factoring out -1 reverses signs."

    YOU DO (15 min)

  • 8x + 12 = 4(___ + ___) 15x - 10 = 5(___ - ___)
  • (-6x + 8) = -2(___ - ___) 12x + 18 = ___(___ + ___)
  • EXTENSION: "Factorise: 6x - 15y + 9"
