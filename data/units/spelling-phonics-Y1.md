# Spelling and Phonics Unit: Digraphs and Blends

**Unit Duration:** 8 weeks × 3 lessons per week = 24 lessons
**Year Level:** Year 1
**Curriculum Link:** Australian Curriculum English v9 - Phonics and Word Knowledge
**AC9E1LY04** — Understand how to use knowledge of spelling to read and write multisyllabic words with regular patterns

**Writing Focus:** Systematic phonics instruction — consonant digraphs (sh, ch, th, wh, ck) and consonant blends (bl, cl, fl, gl, pl, sl, br, cr, dr, fr, gr, pr, tr, st, nd, nt, mp)
**Cold Task:** Pre-assessment (Week 1, Lesson 1) - Spell words with taught phonics patterns
**Hot Task:** Post-assessment (Week 8, Lesson 1) - Same as Cold Task

## Unit Overview

**Learning Intention:**
We are learning to read and spell words that have digraphs (two letters that make one sound) and blends (two or three letters that stay together in a word).

**Success Criteria:**
- I can read and spell words with consonant digraphs: sh, ch, th, wh, ck.
- I can read and spell words with consonant blends: bl, cl, fl, gl, pl, sl, tr, st.
- I can use these words in sentences.
- I can sound out words step by step.

## WEEK 1 — INTRODUCTION AND ASSESSMENT

### Lesson 1: Cold Task (Assessment)
**Time:** 30 minutes | **Type:** Assessment

| Phase | Teacher | Students |
|-------|---------|---------|
| Task (30 min) | Dictation: Teacher says 15 words. Students write them. Words: cat, dog, fish, chip, shop, thin, that, fast, flag, clap, jump, tent, spin, land, belt. Focus: Can students spell CVC words and words with known patterns? | Write words |

---

### Lesson 2: What Are Digraphs? (I Do)
**Time:** 30 minutes | **Type:** Explicit Teaching

| Phase | Teacher | Students |
|-------|---------|---------|
| Hook (5 min) | "Today we are going to learn about SPECIAL SOUNDS. Some sounds are made by TWO letters together." Write: sh — "What sound does sh make?" [shhh]. "When s and h get together, they make ONE sound — sh." | Learn digraph concept |
| I Do (10 min) | Show objects: shell, ship, shoe, fish. "All of these have sh. Can you hear sh in each word?" Say words, have students repeat. | Hear sh |
| You Do (15 min) | Sort pictures: Words that have sh vs words that don't. Circle the sh in each word. | Sort and circle |

**Success Criteria Check:** Can identify sh sound in spoken words.

---

### Lesson 3: sh and ch (We Do)
**Time:** 30 minutes | **Type:** Guided

| Phase | Teacher | Students |
|-------|---------|---------|
| I Do (10 min) | "ch is another digraph. It makes ONE sound." Show: chip, chair, chin, chat. "The mouth does a little 'ch' sound — like you're telling someone to be quiet." | Learn ch |
| We Do (10 min) | Sort: sh words and ch words. Read the words together. Sort pictures. | Sort |
| You Do (10 min) | Write: 3 sh words and 3 ch words. Sound them out. | Write words |

## WEEK 2 — DIGRAPHS

### Lesson 1: th and wh (I Do)
**Time:** 30 minutes | **Type:** Explicit Teaching

| Phase | Teacher | Students |
|-------|---------|---------|
| I Do (10 min) | "th is a digraph. It has TWO sounds — /th/ as in thumb and /th/ as in this." Model both. Show: thumb, that, the, this, with, moth. "Wh makes the /w/ sound." Show: when, what, where, wheel, whip. | Learn th and wh |
| You Do (20 min) | Sort words: th words vs wh words. Write 2 words for each digraph. | Sort and write |

---

### Lesson 2: ck (We Do)
**Time:** 30 minutes | **Type:** Guided

| Phase | Teacher | Students |
|-------|---------|---------|
| I Do (10 min) | "ck is a digraph that makes the /k/ sound. It comes at the END of words." Show: sock, duck, back, neck, tick, rock. "You hear it at the end of short words." | Learn ck |
| We Do (10 min) | Word hunt: Find words with ck in readers or word lists. Write 5 ck words. | Hunt |
| You Do (10 min) | Write sentences with ck words: "I have a black sock." | Write sentences |

---

### Lesson 3: Review and Practice (You Do)
**Time:** 30 minutes | **Type:** Practice

| Phase | Teacher | Students |
|-------|---------|---------|
| You Do (25 min) | Word sort: All 5 digraphs (sh, ch, th, wh, ck). Sort 20 words into 5 groups. Check with partner. | Sort |
| Share (5 min) | Read 2 words from each group | Share |

## WEEK 3 — INTRODUCTION TO BLENDS

### Lesson 1: What Is a Blend? (I Do)
**Time:** 30 minutes | **Type:** Explicit Teaching

| Phase | Teacher | Students |
|-------|---------|---------|
| I Do (10 min) | "A BLEND is two or three letters that BOTH make a sound — they stay together but you hear ALL the sounds." Compare digraph vs blend: SH is one sound (digraph). BL is two sounds (b-l) that stay together (blend). Say bl: /b/ + /l/ = /bl/. Both sounds heard. | Learn blend concept |
| Mini-Lesson (10 min) | l-blends: bl, cl, fl, gl, pl, sl. Say each. Show: blue, clap, flag, glass, plan, sled. | Introduce l-blends |
| You Do (10 min) | Say these words. Can you hear the blend at the start? black, cloud, fly, grape, plate, sled. | Listen |

---

### Lesson 2: l-Blends (We Do)
**Time:** 30 minutes | **Type:** Guided

| Phase | Teacher | Students |
|-------|---------|---------|
| I Do (10 min) | "bl, cl, fl, gl, pl, sl. These all start with /l/ — but you hear the first consonant first, then /l/." Model each: bl-aaack, cl-aap. Show words: black, clap, flag, glass, plan, sled. | Model blends |
| We Do (10 min) | Sort: bl/cl/fl/gl/pl/sl. Given 12 words, sort into 6 groups. | Sort |
| You Do (10 min) | Write 2 words for each l-blend. | Write |

---

### Lesson 3: r-Blends (You Do)
**Time:** 30 minutes | **Type:** Practice

| Phase | Teacher | Students |
|-------|---------|---------|
| I Do (5 min) | r-blends: br, cr, dr, fr, gr, pr, tr. Say each. Show: brown, crab, drum, frog, grape, prize, train. | Introduce r-blends |
| You Do (25 min) | Write r-blend words: Choose 3 r-blends. Write 3 words for each. Read them to a partner. | Write and read |

## WEEK 4 — MORE BLENDS

### Lesson 1: s-Blends (I Do)
**Time:** 30 minutes | **Type:** Explicit Teaching

| Phase | Teacher | Students |
|-------|---------|---------|
| I Do (10 min) | s-blends: sc, sk, sl, sm, sn, sp, st, sw. Show: scar, skip, slip, smell, snap, spin, star, swim. "These all start with /s/ but the /s/ stays with the next sound." | Learn s-blends |
| You Do (20 min) | Word building: Use letter tiles to build: spin, star, slip, spot, step, stamp. Say each sound as you build. | Build words |

---

### Lesson 2: Final Blends (We Do)
**Time:** 30 minutes | **Type:** Guided

| Phase | Teacher | Students |
|-------|---------|---------|
| I Do (10 min) | "Blends can be at the END of words too. Final blends: -st, -nd, -nt, -mp, -sk." Show: fast, hand, tent, jump, ask. | Learn final blends |
| We Do (10 min) | Sort: Initial blend vs final blend. Given words: fast, hand, spin, flag, jump, step — sort into initial/final. | Sort |
| You Do (10 min) | Write 5 words with final blends. | Write |

---

### Lesson 3: Word Building (You Do)
**Time:** 30 minutes | **Type:** Practice

| Phase | Teacher | Students |
|-------|---------|---------|
| You Do (25 min) | Word building activity: Teacher says a word. Students build it with letter tiles or write it. Words: flap, slip, spin, step, stamp, hand, jump, fast, tent, trap. | Build |
| Share (5 min) | Read words to class | Share |

## WEEK 5 — BLENDING AND SEGMENTING

### Lesson 1: Sound Out Words (I Do)
**Time:** 30 minutes | **Type:** Explicit Teaching

| Phase | Teacher | Students |
|-------|---------|---------|
| I Do (10 min) | Model sounding out: bl-aa-ck = /bl/ /a/ /ck/ = black. Show the process: Say each sound, blend together. Use a word with a digraph or blend. | Model blending |
| You Do (20 min) | Sound out these words and write them: ship, chat, that, flag, spin, step, trap, jump, fast, tent. Say each sound, write the letters, check. | Sound and write |

---

### Lesson 2: Build Words (We Do)
**Time:** 30 minutes | **Type:** Guided

| Phase | Teacher | Students |
|-------|---------|---------|
| We Do (15 min) | Build words with letter tiles: bl, cl, fl, gl, pl, sl, br, cr, dr, fr, gr, pr, tr, st, nd, nt, mp. Teacher says a word, students build it. Start simple, increase complexity. | Build |
| You Do (15 min) | Dictation: Teacher says a sentence with words containing blends. Students write: "I can flag the black flag." / "The crab can spin." | Dictation |

---

### Lesson 3: Dictation (You Do)
**Time:** 30 minutes | **Type:** Assessment

| Phase | Teacher | Students |
|-------|---------|---------|
| You Do (30 min) | Dictation sentences: 1. "The frog can jump." 2. "I will grab a red flag." 3. "The crab has a black claw." 4. "She can spin the top." 5. "The train went fast." Check each word. Self-correct. | Write sentences |

## RUBRIC — Phonics and Spelling (Year 1)
**AC9E1LY04** — Read and spell multisyllabic words with regular patterns

| Criterion | A (Excellent) | B (Strong) | C (Satisfactory) | D (Developing) | E (Beginning) |
|-----------|---------------|------------|-----------------|-----------------|---------------|
| **Digraphs** | Reads and spells all digraphs (sh, ch, th, wh, ck) accurately and consistently. | Reads and spells most digraphs accurately. | Reads and spells some digraphs. Errors with some. | Reads and spells few digraphs. Errors frequent. | Cannot identify digraphs. |
| **Blends** | Reads and spells all blends accurately and consistently. | Reads and spells most blends accurately. | Reads and spells some blends. Errors with some. | Reads and spells few blends. Errors frequent. | Cannot identify blends. |
| **Blending** | Blends sounds accurately and smoothly to read words. | Blends with occasional hesitation. | Blends with support. | Blending difficult. | Cannot blend sounds. |
| **Segmentation** | Segments words accurately to spell. | Segments most words. | Segments with support. | Segmentation difficult. | Cannot segment. |
| **Application** | Uses phonics knowledge to read and spell new words independently. | Uses knowledge with some support. | Uses knowledge with guidance. | Limited application. | No application. |

## ADAPTATIONS

### EAL Students
- Pre-teach mouth positions for each sound
- Visual chart with digraph/blend and example words and pictures
- Home language connections — which sounds are similar in your language?
- Extra practice with letter tiles
- One-on-one blending support

### Gifted Students
- Introduce trigraphs (scr, spl, spr, thr) and more complex blends
- Write a poem using blend words
- Create a blend/digraph poster for the classroom
- Research words from other languages that contain these patterns

### Students with Additional Needs
- Reduce to 2 digraphs and 2 blends at a time
- Use letter tiles for all word building
- Provide word bank for all activities
- Extra time for all tasks
- One-on-one blending practice
- Allow partner to check work
