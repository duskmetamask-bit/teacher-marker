## LESSON 1.2 — Rules for Integer Signs

**Duration:** 50 minutes
**Type:** Concept Introduction

---

### Learning Intentions
- I am learning the sign rules for integer multiplication
- I am learning: positive × positive = positive; negative × negative = positive; positive × negative = negative

### Success Criteria
- I can apply sign rules to multiply integers

---

### I DO (15 min)

> "Sign rules for multiplication:"
> "Positive × Positive = Positive: 3×4=12"
> "Negative × Negative = Positive: (-3)×(-4)=12"
> "Positive × Negative = Negative: 3×(-4)=-12"
> "Negative × Positive = Negative: (-3)×4=-12"

> "Same signs → POSITIVE. Different signs → NEGATIVE."

---

### YOU DO (15 min)

1. 5×(-3)=___   (-4)×7=___   (-6)×(-5)=___
2. (-2)×9=___   8×(-3)=___   (-7)×(-8)=___
3. "If 3×(-4)=-12, what is (-3)×4?"
4. EXTENSION: "Prove: (-2)×(-2)×(-2) = -8"

## LESSON 2.1 — Integer Division

**Duration:** 50 minutes
**Type:** Explore

---

### Learning Intentions
- I am learning that integer division follows the same sign rules as multiplication
- I am learning that division is the inverse of multiplication

### Success Criteria
- I can divide integers using sign rules

---

### I DO (15 min)

> "Division sign rules are the SAME as multiplication:"
> "Positive ÷ Positive = Positive: 12÷3=4"
> "Negative ÷ Negative = Positive: (-12)÷(-3)=4"
> "Positive ÷ Negative = Negative: 12÷(-3)=-4"
> "Negative ÷ Positive = Negative: (-12)÷3=-4"

> "Because: if 3×4=12, then 12÷3=4 and 12÷(-3)=-4 and (-12)÷(-3)=4."

---

### YOU DO (15 min)

1. 18÷(-3)=___   (-18)÷3=___   (-18)÷(-3)=___
2. 24÷(-4)=___   (-24)÷6=___   (-24)÷(-4)=___
3. "If 5×(-3)=-15, what is (-15)÷(-3)? What is (-15)÷5?"
4. EXTENSION: "What is 0÷(-5)? What is (-5)÷0?"

## LESSON 3.1 — BEDMAS with Integers

**Duration:** 50 minutes
**Type:** Explain

---

### I DO (15 min)

> "(-3) + 4×(-2)."
> "BEDMAS: multiply before add. 4×(-2)=-8."
> "(-3) + (-8) = -11."

> "(-5) - (-3)×4."
> "Multiply: (-3)×4=-12."
> "(-5) - (-12) = (-5) + 12 = 7."

---

### YOU DO (15 min)

1. 6 + (-3)×2 = ___  (6 + (-6) = 0)
2. (-4) + 5×3 = ___  (-4 + 15 = 11)
3. 8 - (-2)×3 = ___  (8 - (-6) = 8 + 6 = 14)
4. EXTENSION: "(-2)³ - 3×(-4)"

## LESSON 4.1 — Expanding

**Duration:** 50 minutes
**Type:** Explain

---

### I DO (15 min)

> "3(x + 4) = 3x + 12."
> "(-2)(x - 5) = -2x + 10."
> "5(-x + 3) = -5x + 15."

> "When distributing a NEGATIVE: watch the signs!"
> "(-3)(x + 2) = -3x - 6. NOT -3x + 6!"

---

### YOU DO (15 min)

2. (-2)(x + 4) = ___   (-5)(x - 2) = ___
3. 6(-x + 3) = ___
4. EXTENSION: "Expand: 3(x - 2) - 2(x + 4)"

---

## LESSON 4.2 — Factorising

**Duration:** 50 minutes
**Type:** Explain

---

### I DO (12 min)

> "Factorise: 6x - 9 = 3(2x - 3)."

> "Common factor: 3. Divide each term by 3: 6x÷3=2x, 9÷3=3."

> "(-4x + 6) = -2(2x - 3). Note: factoring out -1 reverses signs."

---

### YOU DO (15 min)

1. 8x + 12 = 4(___ + ___)   15x - 10 = 5(___ - ___)
2. (-6x + 8) = -2(___ - ___)   12x + 18 = ___(___ + ___)
3. EXTENSION: "Factorise: 6x - 15y + 9"
